How Long Till...

You're bored. I get it. Why else would you be here, at this website, trying to figure out how many times you have to watch 'Die Hard' until the weekend, end of finals, Christmas, or your birthday? Well thankfully, I've done the hard work. Hi, I'm James. I'll be your calculator today. Put the name of a movie, and an end date (Automatically starts from right now), and see how many times you would have to watch that movie, back-to-back in order to reach your desired date. Or hit random, and let me make those decisions for you. Just remember... There's a lot of movies out there. I.e. Looking for 'Batman' returns about a hundred results and I'm just not willing to do that much work for you, so I've limited it to the top 20 closest matches. If you can't find the right 'Batman' try being more specific - "Batman - The Killing Joke"

tl;dr Type a movie name, hit "Get some movies", pick a date that's special to you, hit "How long till!?".
